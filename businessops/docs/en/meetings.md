# Meeting Cadence (Operating Rhythm)

<!-- BO:BEGIN GENERATED -->
## Principles
- Meetings exist to **decide and unblock**, not to “share status”.
- Every meeting produces **decisions** and **action items** with owners and due dates.
- “No metric, no opinion”: each meeting has 3–7 fixed metrics.

## Cadence (ESSENTIAL)
### Daily — Ops Check-in (10–15 min) *(if you ship/fulfill daily)*
- **Objective:** prevent stockouts, delays, and operational fires.
- **Participants:** OPS (+ Support if any) + FIN (if blocks) + SAL (for critical orders).
- **Fixed agenda:** today’s shipments; stockouts; blockers; returns/damages; 1 blocker to remove.
- **Metrics:** daily OTIF; stockouts; backlog; returns.
- **Outputs:** blocker list + owner + due date.
- **Owner:** Ops owner.

### Weekly — Leadership Review (45–60 min)
- **Objective:** align weekly priorities and decisions.
- **Participants:** DIR + SAL + OPS + FIN.
- **Fixed agenda:** cash 8–12 weeks; pipeline; ops (OTIF/stockouts); risks; action plan.
- **Metrics:** projected cash; forecast revenue; OTIF; stockouts; gross margin (proxy).
- **Outputs:** logged decisions + action plan.
- **Owner:** Founder.

### Weekly — Sales Pipeline (30 min)
- **Objective:** tight follow-ups and reliable forecast.
- **Participants:** SAL (+ DIR if founder-led).
- **Fixed agenda:** top opps; next steps; blockers; non-standard discounts.
- **Outputs:** next steps per deal.

### Biweekly — Cash & Collections (30–45 min)
- **Objective:** disciplined AP/AR.
- **Participants:** FIN + DIR.
- **Fixed agenda:** next 2 weeks payables; receivables; delinquency; credit decisions.
- **Outputs:** approvals and renegotiations.

## Cadence (RECOMMENDED)
### Monthly — Close + KPIs (60–90 min)
- **Objective:** close the numbers and adjust.
- **Participants:** FIN + DIR + OPS + SAL (+ ACC(V) 15 min if needed).
- **Outputs:** 3 decisions + 3 improvements.

### Monthly — Outsourced Vendor Review (30 min)
- **Objective:** enforce SLAs and reduce dependency risk.
- **Participants:** OPS/FIN (depending on vendor) + DIR.
- **Outputs:** improvement backlog.
